Author

Hector Garcia

Héctor García was born in Spain and worked at CERN in Switzerland before moving to Japan, his home for 18 years and counting. While in Tokyo when he is not writing his next book, he makes a living in the IT industry. His popular blog led to his international bestseller A Geek in Japan and later on The Magic of Japan. The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life; translated to 59 languages. Ikigai has the strange honor of being the most translated book ever originally written in Spanish. To this day he has published seven books, the latest one is The Book of Ichigo Ichie.
